GANGSTAS TO GROWERS
RESPONSIVE REDESIGN

The Gangstas to Growers organization serves the Westside of Atlanta by training at-risk youth in agriculture and business. Upon graduation, participants are elligble to become members of the Sweet Sol Cooperative, thereby sharing profits from the sale of Sweet Sol hot sauce. Gangstas to Growers proudly supports and partners with Black farms and businesses throughout the metro-Atlanta area.

Problem Statement

The Gangstas to Growers Problem

Community-minded customers want to financially support the Gangstas to Growers organization, but the site is not mobile responsive and lacks critical information about the program, its participants and how the organization benefits the community. Users are reluctant to contribute because the site lacks legitimacy and authenticity, causing potential donors and customers to be wary of sharing sensitive financial information.

Design Iterations

  • Sketch
    We started by sketching our ideal pages as a paper prototype.
  • Low-fidelity
    Then we converted the sketches to a low-fidelity prototype for testing.
  • Mid-fidelity
    We continued to add colors, adjust fonts, text sizes, images and spacing.
  • Hi-fidelity
    All while maintaining the beautiful aesthetics that G2G already had established, until we had a hi-fidelity product we were happy with.
